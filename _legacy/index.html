02 Doctrine

Where we stand.

01
TENET // SOLIDARITY

Comrades, not customers.

We're a crew, not a clan. No hierarchy, no clout chasing, no main character syndrome. We look out for each other in lobbies and out. Solidarity is the whole point of the room.

02
TENET // COMRADESHIP

Comrades, regardless.

Solidarity isn't a checklist and it isn't a hierarchy. We don't audit who you are at the door and we don't rank who matters most. If you're here in good faith, you're one of us. We've got each other's backs. That's the whole point of the room.

03
TENET // DEFENCE

Zero tolerance, no debate.

Racism, transphobia, homophobia, misogyny, ableism, fascism, cop apologia. Banned on sight. We don't debate human rights with bigots and we don't owe them a hearing.

04
